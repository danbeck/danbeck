Find the matching pairs in this solitaire mahjong game!

alt="Play GreenMahjong in the webbrowser"> The goal is to match open pairs of identical tiles and remove them from the board, exposing the tiles under them for play. The game is finished when all pairs of tiles have been removed from the board or when there are no exposed pairs remaining.
